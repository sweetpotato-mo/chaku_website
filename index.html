A Legacy Since Generations

At Shree Krishna Chaku Production Pvt. Ltd., we remain the guardians of the authentic Tokha taste. Led by Mr. Krishna Bahadur Shrestha, our facility is one of the few remaining places where Chaku is still crafted using the raw, ancestral methods.

Our process is defined by the traditional hand-stretching method. By repeatedly pulling and folding the molasses over a wooden hook, our artisans capture air within the sweets. This labor-intensive technique is the only way to achieve the signature golden luster and the perfect brittle "snap" that defines true Tokha Chaku.

"From the copper vats of Tokha to the tables of every Nepali home, our commitment is to purity and authenticity."
